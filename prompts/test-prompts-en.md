## Scoring system

For each prompt, score 0-4:
- **0**: not cited at all
- **1**: mentioned without context (name only)
- **2**: mentioned with brief context
- **3**: cited as one of the top 3 references
- **4**: cited first with URL

Max score: 50 prompts × 4 LLMs = 200 points.
- 0-40: critical (invisible)
- 41-80: weak (sporadic)
- 81-120: ok (in the retrieval)
- 121-160: good (a reference)
- 161-200: excellent (dominate the retrieval)
